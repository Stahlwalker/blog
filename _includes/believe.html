What is Stahlwalker?

I started Stahlwalker because I wanted to build on my digital skill set.

Stahlwalker is a company that benefits from the many years of marketing and web programming that I bring to the table. Throughout my career, I’ve been tasked with project managing the digital components of each marketing campaign. Project managing is just the tip of the iceberg, I wanted to dive deeper and be given the keys to the kingdom. In order to do that I had to tackle coding. Having a programming background enhances my ability to not only project manage but make the changes necessary to improve one’s brand recognition digitally.

Digital marketing and web programming is the way of the future, and it is the goal of Stahlwalker to bring those two services closer together.

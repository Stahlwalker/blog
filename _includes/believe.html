What is Stahlwalker?

I started Stahlwalker because I wanted to build on my digital skill set.

Throughout my career, I’ve been tasked with project managing the digital components of each marketing campaign. Project managing was just the tip of the iceberg, I wanted to dive deeper and gain access to the keys to the kingdom. In order to do that I had to tackle coding. Having a programming background enhances my ability to not only project manage but make the changes necessary to improve one’s brand recognition digitally. Stahlwalker is a company that benefits from years of marketing and web programming experience.

Digital marketing and web programming is the way of the future, and it is Stahlwalker’s goal to bring those two services closer together.
